Welcome to our Genius Hour project

This project is intended to help students calculate what score they would need to achieve on their finals to maintain a certain rank. In order to do this, we need to collect rank and GPA data from juniors at Plano West. If you would like to participate, please fill out the following form. All data is collected anonymously. Please only submit once per person.
Rank: Your rank as it appears on your mid-year transcript
Total GPA: Your GPA as it appears on your mid-year transcript
Semester GPA: Your GPA for the first semester of junior year (you can calculate that here)
